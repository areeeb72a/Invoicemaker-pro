Help Center & FAQ

Frequently asked questions and guides
How does Offline Mode work?
Invoice Pro stores all your invoices, products, and configurations locally in your browser's secure data warehouse (LocalStorage). It functions 100% without an active internet connection.
How do I generate a PDF invoice?
Click the "Print / Save PDF" button in the preview pane. When the print preview displays, select "Save as PDF" as the Destination in Chrome's print prompt, and check that background graphics are enabled.
Are my payment QR codes secure?
Yes. The QR Code generator script is bundled locally inside this extension, meaning your payment info (e.g., UPI ID) is converted directly into an SVG/canvas QR code without hitting external servers.
How do I restore my backup?
Go to Settings -> Click "Restore Data", select your downloaded JSON backup file, and confirm. All records will be imported instantly.
